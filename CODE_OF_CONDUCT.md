# Code of Conduct

## Our promise

macowl is a small and friendly project. Everyone is welcome here, no matter
your background, your experience level, or where you come from.

## How we behave

Please be kind and patient. A few simple things:

- Be respectful to other people.
- Give helpful feedback, not rude feedback.
- Accept that people make mistakes, and help them learn.
- Keep the discussion about the project, not about the person.

## What is not allowed

- Rude, hateful or insulting comments.
- Personal attacks or harassment of any kind.
- Sharing someone's private information without permission.

## If there is a problem

If you see behaviour that breaks these rules, please open an issue or contact
the maintainer. Reports will be looked at seriously and kept private.

This Code of Conduct is adapted in spirit from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
